The Problem

Running an Etsy store manually demands 20+ hours per week: trend research, product design, SEO copywriting, customer replies, Pinterest marketing. Solo sellers burn out before they scale. Generic automation tools don't understand Etsy's nuances — and hiring a team kills margin before it's earned.

The Solution

AstroFur Syndicate deploys a coordinated fleet of 7 specialized AI agents — each with a distinct role, personality, and visual identity — to autonomously research trends, create products, optimize listings, run marketing, and handle customer engagement. The human sets strategy; the crew executes everything else, 24/7.
